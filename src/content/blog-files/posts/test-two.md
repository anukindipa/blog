## cauchy sequence

A sequence $x_{1}, x_{2}, x_{3}\dots$ of real numbers is cauchy if 
$$
\begin{align*}
\forall \epsilon>0,
\exists N \in \mathbb{N} : \forall n,m &\ge N \Rightarrow |x_{n} - x_{m}| < \epsilon
\end{align*}
$$
